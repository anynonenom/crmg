How to Use

Step by Step

Getting started with the EIDEN Group dashboard.

  1. 1
    Log in at the root URL
    Open crmg.eiden-group.com in your browser. Enter your email and password on the EIDEN Group login screen. Only superadmin credentials work here.
  2. 2
    Read the Global Overview
    You land on the Global Overview dashboard. At the top: 4 KPI tiles showing total orgs, bookings, students, and combined revenue. Below: organization cards for Lunja Village and EducaZen Kids with their live stats.
  3. 3
    Switch into an organization
    Use the 🌴 dropdown in the top header to filter data by organization. Click "Lunja Village" or "EducaZen Kids" to see only that org's data in Contacts, Bookings, and Tasks pages.
  4. 4
    Manage organizations
    Click Organizations in the sidebar to create a new client org, edit their name/email/password, or remove one. Each org gets their own branded login page.
  5. 5
    Seed demo data
    Click the Seed Data button in the top bar to populate organizations, guests, bookings, and tasks with sample data for demonstration purposes.
  6. 6
    Monitor notifications
    Click the bell icon in the top right to see all real-time notifications across all organizations — new enrollments at EducaZen, new bookings at Lunja, etc.
