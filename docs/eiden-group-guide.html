How to Use

Step by Step

Getting started with the EIDEN Group dashboard.

  1. 1
    Log in at the root URL
    Open crmg.eiden-group.com. Enter admin@eiden-group.com and your password on the EIDEN Group login screen. Only superadmin credentials work here.
  2. 2
    Client Monitoring Dashboard
    You land on the Client Monitoring page — 4 KPI tiles (Partners, Lunja Revenue, EducaZen Revenue, Combined Total) plus live health cards for each client showing their key numbers at a glance.
  3. 3
    Monitor Lunja Village
    In the left sidebar, under Client Partners, click Lunja Village. The main area loads 5 tabs: Overview · Guests · Bookings · Tasks · Revenue — all live, all read-only.
  4. 4
    Monitor EducaZen Kids
    Click EducaZen Kids in the sidebar. The EducaZen dashboard loads with all their tabs — students, classes, attendance, payments, staff — live data.
  5. 5
    Manage client organizations
    Click Organizations in the sidebar to add a new client, edit an existing org's name or email, or remove one. New clients get their own branded portal URL.
  6. 6
    Return to global overview
    Click Overview at the top of the sidebar at any time to return to the Client Monitoring dashboard and see the combined view of all partners.
