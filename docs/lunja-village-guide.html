Features

Admin Capabilities

Every tool you need to run resort operations.

📊
Dashboard
Live stats: active guests, bookings, open tasks, and revenue. Arrivals table and revenue bar chart for last 7 months.
👥
Contacts
Full guest database — VIP, Regular, New. Add, edit, search, and filter contacts. Track stays, spend, and status.
📅
Bookings
Create and manage stays, events, and dining reservations. Filter by status (Active, Pending, Completed) and track check-in/out dates.
✅
Tasks
Assign operational tasks to departments (Housekeeping, Maintenance, Concierge, F&B). Mark urgent, set due times, filter by team.
💰
Revenue
Monthly revenue chart, breakdown by booking type, and total earnings tracking across all bookings.
🎯
Leads / Pipeline
Sales pipeline from D0 to D4. Track potential clients, their source, deal value, and move them through the funnel.
📆
Calendar
Monthly calendar view of all bookings and tasks. Spot conflicts and plan ahead at a glance.
🔔
Notifications
Real-time bell notifications for new bookings, tasks, and important events. Mark all as read from the dropdown.

Getting Started

Step by Step

  1. 1
    Open the Lunja Village portal
    Go to crmg.eiden-group.com/lunja-village. You'll see the warm cream Lunja Village login page.
  2. 2
    Sign in as Admin
    Enter lunja@eiden-group.com and password123. You're taken directly to the Lunja dashboard.
  3. 3
    Add your first guest
    Click Contacts in the sidebar → + New Contact. Fill in name, email, type (VIP/Regular/New), and status.
  4. 4
    Create a booking
    Click Bookings → + New Booking. Select guest name, type (Stay/Event/Dining), check-in and check-out dates, and amount.
  5. 5
    Assign tasks to staff
    Click Tasks → + New Task. Set the task name, assign a department, due time, and mark as urgent if needed.
  6. 6
    Track revenue
    Click Revenue to see the earnings chart by month and a breakdown of all booking amounts.
  7. 7
    Log out
    Click the Log Out button at the bottom of the sidebar. Your session is cleared and you return to the login page.
